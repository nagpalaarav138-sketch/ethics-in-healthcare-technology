# Open Ethical Questions for the Future

## Overview
As healthcare technology continues to evolve, new ethical challenges will emerge. From genetic editing to predictive analytics, future innovations will test existing ethical frameworks.

## Key Themes
- Increasing automation in clinical decision-making
- Expansion of remote and digital healthcare
- Growing role of private technology companies in public health

## Ethical Tensions
Innovation often outpaces regulation. Policymakers, clinicians, and technologists must collaborate to ensure that progress does not come at the expense of patient welfare or social trust.

## Looking Ahead
Rather than seeking final answers, ethical inquiry in healthcare technology should remain adaptive and ongoing. Continuous reflection is necessary to ensure that technology serves human values.

## Open Questions
- How can ethical oversight keep pace with innovation?
- What values should guide the future of healthcare technology?
- Who should have a voice in shaping ethical standards?
