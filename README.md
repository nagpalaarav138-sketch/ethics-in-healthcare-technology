# Ethics in Healthcare Technology

This repository explores ethical questions that arise as technology becomes more deeply embedded in healthcare systems.  
The goal is not to provide answers, but to analyze trade-offs, risks, and responsibilities.

## Why this project?
Healthcare technologies such as AI diagnostics, electronic health records, and algorithmic decision systems offer enormous benefits â€” but also introduce ethical challenges related to safety, bias, privacy, and equity.

This project breaks down these issues in short, focused essays.

## Scope
- Educational and analytical
- Non-clinical
- No medical advice

## Structure
Each essay:
- Introduces the technology
- Identifies ethical risks
- Discusses real-world implications
- Raises open-ended questions
